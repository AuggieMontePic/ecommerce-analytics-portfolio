## About This Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates end-to-end data analytics capabilities applied to real-world e-commerce data. Two projects built on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset showcase SQL query writing, data modeling, and dashboard development across two industry-standard BI tools.

Each project follows a complete analytics workflow:
**Raw Data → BigQuery SQL → Dashboard Visualization → Business Insights**

---

## Projects

### Project 1 — Brazilian E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics
**Tool:** Looker Studio  
**Focus:** Sales performance, customer segmentation, and geographic distribution  
**Queries:** 5 BigQuery SQL queries  
**Dashboard:** 3-page interactive Looker Studio report

Key findings:
- $15.4M total revenue across 96.5K orders (2017–2018)
- 59.1% of revenue generated by the top 25% of customers (High-Value One-Timers)
- São Paulo dominates with $2.7M revenue — nearly double second-place Rio de Janeiro
- Near-zero repeat purchase rate reveals an acquisition-dependent business model
- Orders delivered an average of 12+ days ahead of estimated delivery date

### Project 2 — Tech E-Commerce Product & Operations Analytics
**Tool:** Power BI  
**Focus:** Tech product performance, delivery operations, and seller analysis  
**Queries:** 4 BigQuery SQL queries  
**Dashboard:** 3-page Power BI report filtered to 7 tech categories

Key findings:
- $1.83M revenue across 15K orders in tech categories
- Computer Accessories dominates with $911K revenue but Computers lead on margin at 9.56%
- 92% on-time delivery rate across tech orders
- Only 10.08% of 476 sellers qualify as Elite — significant quality concentration at the top
- Average seller review score of 3.9/5 indicates room for seller quality improvement
